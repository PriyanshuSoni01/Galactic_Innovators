ABOUT

Welcome to Galactic Innovators, where cutting-edge technology meets the mysteries of space. We specialize in enhancing images of lunar craters captured by Chandrayaan using advanced Generative Adversarial Networks (GAN). Our mission is to unlock the hidden details of the Moon's surface, particularly in the permanently shadowed regions, providing unprecedented clarity and insights. At Galactic Innovators, we are committed to pushing the boundaries of space exploration and image processing, making the unseen seen and the unknown known.
